5.Question
What is the 'Curse of Knowledge' and how does it affect
communication?
Answer:The 'Curse of Knowledge' refers to the difficulty of
conveying information clearly to others when one has
extensive knowledge about a topic. This often leads to
insiders using jargon or complex explanations that confuse
outsiders.

clarify their mission. By continuously asking 'why' their
initiative is important, they can distill their message into
compelling emotional appeals that resonate with a wider
audience.
