About the book
In a world brimming with relentless noise and fleeting
attention spans, how does a business craft a message that not
only gets heard but deeply resonates? Enter **Building a
StoryBrand** by Donald Miller—a transformative guide that
reframes the art of marketing through the timeless principles
of storytelling. By positioning your customer as the hero and
your brand as the reliable guide, Miller unveils a clear,
seven-part framework that cuts through the clutter, ensuring
your story captivates and converts. Whether you're a seasoned
entrepreneur or just starting out, this book is your roadmap to
clarifying your message, connecting more authentically with
your audience, and ultimately driving your business to new
heights. Dive in and discover how the power of narrative can
transform your brand and your bottom line.

About the author
Donald Miller is a celebrated author, public speaker, and
business expert, renowned for his profound impact on the
fields of marketing and personal development. He first gained
widespread recognition with his memoir "Blue Like Jazz," a
New York Times bestseller that captivated readers with its
introspective and authentic storytelling. Donald's
entrepreneurial spirit led him to establish StoryBrand, a
company dedicated to helping businesses clarify their
messaging through a framework that positions customers as
the heroes of their own stories. His innovative approach has
empowered countless organizations to connect more deeply
with their audiences, fueling growth and success. As a
sought-after consultant and keynote speaker, Donald continues
to share his insights on narrative, branding, and leadership,
making him a pivotal figure in contemporary business
strategy.
