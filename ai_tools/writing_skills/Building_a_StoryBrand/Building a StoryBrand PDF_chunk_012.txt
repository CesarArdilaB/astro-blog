Implementing StoryBrand in Marketing - Practical
Applications and Strategies
Applying the StoryBrand framework across various
marketing channels is an essential step to ensure that your
brand message remains consistent and compelling. To begin
with, every brand needs to audit its existing marketing
materials. This means scrutinizing websites, email
campaigns, social media profiles, brochures, and
advertisements to ensure they reflect the clarity and
customer-centric focus of the StoryBrand framework.
One practical application is revamping your website. Your
website is often the first point of contact with potential
customers, and it must instantly communicate how you can
solve their problems and improve their lives. The homepage
should clearly identify the customer's problem, introduce
your brand as the guide, show empathy and authority, outline

Squatty Potty, a company that drastically improved its online
sales after implementing the StoryBrand framework,

Email marketing, when aligned with the StoryBrand
framework, can also yield significant results. Each email
should continue to emphasize the customer's journey,
providing valuable content that guides them toward a
solution. Personalized emails that target specific pain points
and highlight success stories can reinforce the emotional
connection and keep your audience engaged.
Social media platforms afford another opportunity to apply
the StoryBrand principles. Social channels should
consistently reflect the story you are telling, with every post
reinforcing how your brand helps customers achieve success
or avoid failure. For example, Nike effectively uses social
media to tell inspirational stories featuring athletes
overcoming challenges, which mirrors the hero's journey and
positions Nike as the trusted guide.
