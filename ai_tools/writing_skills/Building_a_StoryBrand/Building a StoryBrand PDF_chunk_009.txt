Customers to Take the Next Step

guiding potential customers toward making a decision and
taking the next step in their journey. In the "Building a
StoryBrand" framework by Donald Miller, the CTA serves as
a direct pathway for customers to engage with your brand,
ultimately leading them to a solution to their problems. This
part of the summary delves into the importance of CTAs,
how to craft them, and strategies to enhance their
effectiveness.
