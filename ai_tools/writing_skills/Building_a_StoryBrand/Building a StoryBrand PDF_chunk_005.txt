Chapter 3 : The Role of the Guide -
Establishing Your Brand's Authority
In the StoryBrand framework, the role of the guide is pivotal
in establishing your brand's authority and credibility. This
part of the framework is based on the crucial understanding
that while the customer is the hero of the story, your brand
must position itself as a reliable and supportive guide who
has the expertise and empathy to help them succeed.
A fundamental element of storytelling, from ancient myths to
contemporary movies, is the presence of a mentor or guide
who assists the hero on their journey. In the context of
branding, this concept translates to presenting your brand as
the mentor who can guide customers towards achieving their
desired outcomes. Establishing your brand as an empathetic
figure involves demonstrating that you genuinely understand
and care about the customer’s pains, struggles, and
aspirations. This empathy needs to be evident in your
messaging, ensuring that your audience feels heard and
valued.
Equally important is communicating your brand’s authority.
