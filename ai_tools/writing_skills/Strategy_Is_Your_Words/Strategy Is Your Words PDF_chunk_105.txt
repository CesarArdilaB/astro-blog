Chapter 12 | Practice Plays the Bass| Q&A
1.Question
What role does practice play in strategy and creativity?
Answer:Practice serves as the foundational 'bass
line' in a strategist's life—steady and reliable. It
grounds you, ensuring that your creative expression
is rooted in genuine effort rather than fleeting highs.
While achievements may feel exhilarating (like the
treble in music), it's the consistent practice that
brings true meaning and sustainability. It helps you
interface with your creative spirit, cultivating
personal growth and deepening understanding.
2.Question
How can I incorporate practice into my daily life?
Answer:Incorporate practice by creating small and

[PÁGINA 382]

manageable rituals such as carrying a notebook for
observations, engaging in regular discussions about ideas, or
setting aside time for creative endeavors, regardless of how
trivial they seem. This conscious effort can happen
anywhere—from a subway journey to a break during
lunch—allowing your thoughts to flow and evolve.
3.Question
Why is deliberate practice emphasized over casual
practicing?
Answer:Deliberate practice is highlighted because it goes
beyond mere repetition; it focuses on targeted skill
development through structured, meaningful engagement. By
breaking down necessary skills and receiving constructive
feedback, you accelerate your progress and deepen your
competency. This method distinguishes elite performers from
those who only engage in surface-level practice.
4.Question
What is the significance of treating practice as a way of
life?

[PÁGINA 383]

Answer:Viewing practice as a philosophy rather than just a
task elevates your approach towards learning and creativity.
It enables you to integrate practice into every facet of your
existence—turning mundane moments into opportunities for
growth. This mindset ensures a more profound, satisfying
connection to your work and your values.
5.Question
How does practice contribute to finding peace in one’s
life?
Answer:Practice fosters peace by shifting focus from
immediate results to the joy of the process. It allows you to
approach challenges as opportunities for learning while
distancing yourself from external pressures and expectations.
Engaging in regular practice gives you a meditative space to
express your creativity, promoting a sense of tranquility and
clarity.
