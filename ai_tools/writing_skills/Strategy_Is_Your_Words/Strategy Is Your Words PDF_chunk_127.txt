Chapter 28 | Operating Words| Q&A
1.Question
How do the words we use influence our personal and
professional identities?
Answer:Words are not just superficial constructs;
they shape our thoughts and actions profoundly.
The language we employ, especially those we use
internally when conversing with ourselves, sets the
tone for our confidence and self-perception. For
instance, positive affirmations can bolster our
self-esteem and impact the way we present ourselves
in meetings, while negative self-talk can undermine
our performance and creativity. By becoming aware
of our operating words, we build a foundation that
helps us navigate both our careers and self-concept
effectively.
2.Question
What is the significance of remembering oneself beyond a
career in strategy?

[PÁGINA 456]

Answer:In a fleeting strategy career, it's vital to maintain a
sense of self that transcends job titles. By honoring our past
contributions and personal growth, we can create a resilient
identity that thrives outside of professional roles. For
example, after stepping away from a job, an individual who
has internally celebrated their achievements and capabilities
can re-enter the workforce with renewed confidence and
clarity about their value, ensuring their self-worth isn't tied
solely to their job, but rather to a broader understanding of
their purpose in life.
