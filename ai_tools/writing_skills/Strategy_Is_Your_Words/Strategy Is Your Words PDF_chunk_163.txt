5.Question
Why should the language used in generating insights be
direct and straightforward?
Answer:Direct language ensures clarity, making insights
more impactful and action-oriented. Complex terminology
can obscure meaning, while simple words resonate better and
facilitate stronger connections to the audience.
6.Question
How can favorite authors influence the writing of insights
in workshops?
Answer:Participants can look to their favorite writers for
inspiration, adopting their style and flair to invigorate their
insights. Reading and copying a paragraph from a preferred
work can help participants shift thoughts from vague to
specific, enhancing authenticity in their expressions.
