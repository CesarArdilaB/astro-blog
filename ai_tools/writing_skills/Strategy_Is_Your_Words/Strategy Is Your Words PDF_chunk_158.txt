What are insights and how do they function in human
experience?

words to hunches and thoughts that people may feel
but can't articulate. They help name feelings and

world. This naming of experiences offers individuals

2.Question
How do insights affect personal transformations?
Answer:Insights can prompt personal transformations by
challenging preconceived notions and behaviors. For
example, if an individual realizes that the Romantic notion of
love is flawed, they may reconsider their relationships and
emotional dependencies, thereby reshaping their approach to
love and personal connections.

4.Question
In what ways can insights lead to business success?
Answer:Insights can lead to business success by creating
winning strategies that resonate with clients and consumers.
A well-articulated insight can increase client retention, spark
innovative product ranges, and fundamentally alter business
relationships, often resulting in promotions and accolades for
those who discover and leverage them.

experiences to bond over shared realizations. This can lead to
conversations, online communities, and collaborative efforts
to address the issues uncovered by the insights.
