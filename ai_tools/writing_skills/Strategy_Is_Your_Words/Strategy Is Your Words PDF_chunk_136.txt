6.Question
Is 'big' or 'small' useful categories for ideas?
Answer:The categorization of ideas as 'big' or 'small' can be
limiting. It can detract from the appreciation of ideas' true
potential and their ability to create value. Focusing on the
effectiveness and relevance of the ideas regardless of their
size might be more beneficial.
7.Question
How can we better evaluate the worth of an idea?
Answer:Instead of labeling ideas by size, evaluating them
based on their impact, relevance, creativity, and ability to
solve real problems can provide a richer understanding of
their value. This approach encourages a broader perspective
on innovation.
