The Transition to Possibilities
After grasping the existing elements, strategists venture into
the realm of possibilities—how things could exist in the
future. This creative aspect includes developing strategies
and propositions that bring existing elements together in
meaningful ways, highlighting the artistic nature of strategy.
Challenges of Impractical Strategy
The text warns against the pitfalls of impractical strategy that
becomes overly intellectual and detached from reality. Such
strategies can become bogged down in abstract ideas and
convoluted language, leading to frustration and confusion
among colleagues.
