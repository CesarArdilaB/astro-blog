About the book
"Strategy Is Your Words" by Mark Pollard is a unique guide
tailored for those who thrive on introspection and empathy in
the fast-paced world of advertising, marketing, and media.
This book resonates with strategists grappling with the
pressures of crafting creative briefs and driving impactful
workshops in environments that often overlook their
complexities. Celebrated as a touchstone for modern
strategists, it dives into the emotional currents that influence
their thoughts—exploring terms like "impostor syndrome" and
"clarity"—before transitioning into actionable strategies to
enhance their work. The second half of the book provides a
practical toolkit, featuring techniques such as problem
statements, insight writing, and the innovative Four Points
Framework. Complete with over 20 techniques, examples, and
illustrations, this resource is designed for strategists seeking
clarity and creativity, making it an indispensable companion
for every project.

About the author
Mark Pollard is a seasoned strategist and creative leader
known for his innovative approach to the intersection of
strategy, creativity, and storytelling. With over a decade of
experience across various industries, including advertising and
digital marketing, Pollard has worked with some of the world's
leading brands, helping them navigate complex challenges and
craft impactful narratives. His expertise lies in transforming
abstract ideas into actionable strategies, and he is renowned for
his ability to distill intricate concepts into compelling
communications. Through his book "Strategy Is Your Words,"
Pollard shares insights drawn from his diverse experiences,
aiming to empower professionals in using language as a
strategic tool for clarity and influence in their work.
