5.Question
What is the significance of communicating the unique
advantage of a product?
Answer:Communicating the unique advantage is crucial as it
must resonate in the audience’s minds, emphasizing how the
product is better and more compelling compared to
competitors.
6.Question
What is advised to avoid in strategic discussions and
why?
Answer:It is advised to avoid simply listing bullet points, as
that can indicate an aversion to facing tough questions about
what truly matters to the audience.
7.Question
What are some key questions to ask during consumer
interviews?

[PÁGINA 579]

Answer:Key questions include: Why did you buy this
product instead of another? How do you use it? Did anything
surprise you? What’s the best feature? How would you
convince a friend to buy it?
8.Question
What value do online consumer reviews provide
according to the author?
Answer:Online consumer reviews can offer insights into how
consumers perceived the product before purchase and their
opinions after use. However, the author warns against relying
solely on word clouds from those reviews.
9.Question
What needs to be established between the brand and the
product for effective communication?
Answer:There needs to be a strong connection or 'sinew'
between the brand and the product; otherwise, the
communication will not be credible.

Chapter 56 | The Pyramid of Advantage| Q&A
1.Question
What makes a brand's advantage 'unfair'?
Answer:An unfair advantage is something special
that a company has which is difficult for competitors
to replicate. An example would be a patent that
protects a unique technology or product design that
gives the company a distinct edge in the
marketplace.
