1.Question
What does going public mean in the context of strategy
and publishing?
Answer:Going public refers to the act of sharing
your thoughts, insights, and professional identity
with the broader world. This process involves more
than just seeking recognition; it entails authentic
engagement with larger audiences—friends,
colleagues, and the overall industry—through
various mediums such as online portfolios, articles,
and speaking engagements. By doing so, strategists
not only seek personal fulfillment but also aim to
foster connections with potential job opportunities
and create a meaningful impact within their field.
