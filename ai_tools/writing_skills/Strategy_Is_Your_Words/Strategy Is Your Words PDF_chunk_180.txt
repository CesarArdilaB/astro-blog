Chapter 67 | Communications Frameworks –A
Short Word| Q&A
1.Question
What is a communications framework, and why is it
important for campaigns?
Answer:A communications framework is a
structured approach that helps a campaign idea
manifest effectively by aligning the idea with the
right media and audience. It is crucial because it
creates synergy between the concept and its
presentation, ensuring that the campaign resonates
with the intended audience and efficiently utilizes
resources.
2.Question
How should one choose media channels for a campaign?
Answer:You should shortlist media channels that the target
audience uses and that fit within your budget before crafting
the creative brief. The choice should be informed by both
marketing science and past campaign insights, while still
allowing for the creativity and intuition of the campaign idea

3.Question
What questions should you ask when developing a
personal 'communications plan' for dating?
Answer:1. What do I want? 2. What do the people I want
want? 3. Where do they go to get it? 4. What will I say to get
it? 5. How will I behave to get it? 6. How will I know if I’m
getting it? 7. How do I keep getting it?
4.Question
Can you give an example of how to apply a
communications framework to a campaign idea?
Answer:For the 'Play with Death' campaign, you could use a
three-act structure where: 1. In the Tease phase, create
mysterious advertisements that intrigue audiences (e.g.,
cryptic social media posts). 2. In the Launch phase, host a
grand event that dramatically showcases the concept (e.g., a
sandbox exhibit). 3. In the Sustain phase, engage audiences
with ongoing interactions or challenges related to the
campaign messaging to maintain interest.

5.Question
What role does understanding objections play in
developing a communications plan?
Answer:Understanding objections is essential as it allows
you to anticipate potential hesitations from your audience. By
addressing these objections directly in your campaign's
messaging, you can reassure potential customers and guide
them toward overcoming their fears or doubts about the
product.
6.Question
Why might a campaign still explore extreme or
unconventional channels?
Answer:Exploring unconventional channels can lead to
unexpected insights or innovative ideas. Sometimes, the
more extreme options might resonate with the audience in
ways that traditional methods cannot, sparking engagement
and conversation around the campaign theme.
