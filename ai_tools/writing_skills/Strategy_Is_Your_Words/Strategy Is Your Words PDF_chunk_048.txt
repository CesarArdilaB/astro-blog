The Types of Ideas in Detail: A Walk-Through
A business idea is a novel concept that defines what a
company does. Often starting as a product feature, a
successful business idea needs to evolve into something with
potential for scalability and repeatable processes.
Product Features versus Full Products
- A business idea might begin as a product feature that
addresses a problem in existing offerings.
- Example: A website for Halloween movies may be a feature
rather than a full product, as it could be easily adopted by
existing services.
Developing Compelling Ideas
- Entrepreneurs should consider how to combine features into
a more compelling idea.
