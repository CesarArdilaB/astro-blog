5.Question
How is the theme of 'play' contrasted with 'running'
throughout the chapter?
Answer:'Play' represents a deeper, more meaningful
engagement in tasks that allow for creativity and enjoyment,
while 'running' signifies superficial activity that lacks
direction and purpose. This contrast emphasizes that true
success—whether in sports or in life—comes from playing
the game with heart and strategy rather than simply
participating in it through endless busyness.
