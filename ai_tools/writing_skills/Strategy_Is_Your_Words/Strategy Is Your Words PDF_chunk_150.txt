1.Question
What does the New York Knicks situation illustrate about
business independence from success?
Answer:The New York Knicks exemplify a business
decoupled from the need to win, demonstrating that
financial survival can exist alongside a history of
poor performance or consistent losses. Their ability
to maintain revenue despite lack of championships
shows a unique form of business resilience,
suggesting that teams can operate successfully even
when they consistently disappoint their fans.
