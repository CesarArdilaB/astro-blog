What is the DIKW Pyramid and how does it relate to
understanding data, information, knowledge, and
wisdom?
Answer:The DIKW Pyramid illustrates the hierarchical
relationship between data, information, knowledge, and
wisdom. Data, at the base, consists of raw facts; when these
are processed or contextualized, they transform into
information. As information is further interpreted, it becomes
knowledge, which can then lead to wisdom—an
understanding that transcends mere data analysis. This
progression emphasizes the importance of discerning depth
and meaning beyond surface-level statistics.

4.Question
What are the dangers of 'data worship'?
Answer:Data worship can lead to a controlled approach to
decision-making where individual creativity is suppressed in
favor of quantifiable results. This mindset turns people into
mere repositories of data instead of allowing them to engage
in critical thinking, which is essential for artistic and strategic
development, much like playing a piano with one finger
restricts musical expression.
