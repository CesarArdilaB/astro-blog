discussing insights?

of insights, making them resonate more profoundly with

Chapter 49 | The Difficulty with Insights| Q&A
1.Question
What are insights, and how do they differ from numbers,
reports, and facts?
Answer:Insights are the underlying reasons why
numbers exist; they aren’t just data points, but
rather, they reflect aspects of humanity behind the
numbers. Unlike reports that compile data and facts
that provide information, insights delve deeper,
aiming to understand motivations and behaviors.
They integrate the raw data into meaningful
statements about human experiences.
2.Question
Why is brevity important when it comes to insights?
Answer:Brevity is crucial because it allows insights to
remain impactful and memorable. A concise insight can
resonate longer with an audience compared to a lengthy
explanation, ensuring it is understood and can be easily
recalled.
