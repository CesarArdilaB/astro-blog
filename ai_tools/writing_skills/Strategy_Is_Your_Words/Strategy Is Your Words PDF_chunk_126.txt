4.Question
How does one sit in patience?
Answer:Sitting in patience involves allowing oneself to be
present with discomfort and uncertainty, resisting the
temptation to act hastily or impulsively, and reflecting on
what truly matters.
5.Question
What are the four fantasies that complicate patience?
Answer:The four fantasies are: 1. Desire - unclear or adopted
desires hinder clarity; 2. Time - misconceptions about how
time influences outcomes; 3. Judging circumstances - biases
affecting how we perceive progress and success; 4.
Predicting the future - the fantasy of being able to foresee
future outcomes based on current actions.

7.Question
Why is patience considered a virtue until it isn't?
Answer:Patience is virtue in situations that require thoughtful
waiting; however, it can morph into stagnation when it leads
to inaction or acceptance of negative circumstances.
