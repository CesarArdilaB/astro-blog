6.Question
What are the distinctions between vision and mission
statements?
Answer:Vision statements articulate how the world will
change because of a brand’s efforts, envisioning a future
impact. Mission statements detail the approach and activities
the brand will undertake to achieve this change. Keeping
them concise helps to communicate them more effectively,
grounding lofty ideas in tangible actions.

10.Question
What common pitfalls should strategists watch out for
when crafting vision and mission statements?
Answer:Common pitfalls include using jargon, being overly
vague, or creating statements that are too lengthy and filled
with unnecessary adjectives. Strategists should aim for
succinct, clear statements that convey the essence of their
purpose without falling into corporate cliches, ensuring they
resonate with their intended audience.
