Chapter 37 | An Example: Hello Hallow— A
Company In the Business 0f Fear| Q&A
1.Question
What is the concept behind Hello Hallow's business idea?
Answer:Hello Hallow is designed to serve Halloween
to consumers year-round through a subscription
service that delivers themed boxes monthly, filled
with Halloween-related items like ghouls, goblins,
masks, and games.
2.Question
How does Hello Hallow define its brand essence?
Answer:The brand essence of Hello Hallow is "Sexy Scary",
which reflects its aim to make fear fun and entertaining for
its audience.
3.Question
What promise does Hello Hallow make to its customers?
Answer:Hello Hallow promises to help people have fun with
fear, providing entertaining experiences that challenge them
to engage with spooky themes in a playful way.
4.Question

[PÁGINA 497]

What does the tagline 'Play with Death' signify?
Answer:The tagline 'Play with Death' encapsulates Hello
Hallow's brand idea of encouraging customers to confront
and enjoy their fears in a lighthearted manner, much like
children would play with toys.
5.Question
What are some creative campaign ideas for Hello
Hallow?
Answer:The first year's campaign, 'Play with Death', features
surreal ads showing toddlers with adult faces in a sandbox
surrounded by scary items. The second year's campaign,
'Play with Death from Your Deathbed', evolves the concept
by showing older adult faces on toddlers, continuing the
theme of playful confrontation with fear.
6.Question

engaging customers?

brand?
