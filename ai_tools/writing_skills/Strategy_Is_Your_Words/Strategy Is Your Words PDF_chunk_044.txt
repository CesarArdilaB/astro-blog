Not All Thoughts Are Ideas
The use of the word "idea" is often mismanaged, which can
lead to confusion. It can mean different things in various
contexts, such as proposing a suggestion, claiming credit,
insulting someone's understanding, or expressing
astonishment. Misusing "idea" rather than "thought" can
obscure real creativity, as an idea should represent a unique
combination of typically unrelated concepts.
