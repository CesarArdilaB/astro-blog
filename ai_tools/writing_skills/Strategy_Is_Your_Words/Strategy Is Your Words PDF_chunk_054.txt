The Four Points framework consists of four key sentences
that collectively support a unified theme. The sentences
should embody strong ideas that resonate, avoiding weak or
disjointed thoughts. It helps teams collaborate effectively
towards a common goal by establishing a clear business issue
and understanding the target audience.
