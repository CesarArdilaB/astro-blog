5.Question
What distinguishes an advertising idea from a campaign
idea?
Answer:An advertising idea is broader and exists across all
brand communications, while a campaign idea is often more
focused and short-term, adapting to specific needs or
promotions yet still aligned to the overarching advertising
idea.
6.Question
Can you provide an example of transforming a campaign
idea over time?
Answer:The Hello Hallow example illustrates this idea:
Initially launching with the notion of 'Play with Death,' the
campaign evolved into 'Play with Death from Your Deathbed'
to target retirement-home residents, thus maintaining the core
concept while adapting to new audiences and contexts.

7.Question
How does Hello Hallow embody the concept of
non-advertising ideas?
Answer:Hello Hallow could create initiatives like community
games aimed at rehabilitating opioid addicts based on the

8.Question
What is a content idea and how does it differ from an
advertisement?
Answer:A content idea respects the audience's time and
interest, presenting something people actively seek, such as a
web series or engaging storytelling experience. In contrast,
advertisements typically interrupt viewers with promotional
messages that might not interest them.
9.Question
What role does creativity play in executing advertising
ideas?
Answer:Creativity is crucial in ensuring that advertising
ideas resonate with their intended audience across different

[PÁGINA 494]

channels, leading to impactful campaigns that are engaging
and respectful of the platform used without falling into

10.Question
What key questions should entrepreneurs consider when
developing their ideas?
Answer:Entrepreneurs should reflect on their idea's
scalability, uniqueness, potential for future products, and
whether their brand essence and purpose genuinely connect
with the emotions and needs of their targeted audience.
