4.Question
What are 'obedient truths' and 'disobedient truths'?
Answer:Obedient truths are accepted, comfortable answers
that require little questioning (e.g., 'Handcrafted Sandwiches'
implying quality). In contrast, disobedient truths challenge
norms and demand deeper examination, starting with 'No',
encouraging the questioning of established beliefs and
exploring alternative perspectives.

apart in competitive environments.

9.Question
Can you provide an example of how a banal truth can
transform into an edge truth?
Answer:A banal truth like 'everyone reads books with their
hands' could transform into an edge truth if someone
discovers a method to read a book in sixty seconds while
fully committing it to memory—a skill few possess, thus
providing a competitive advantage.
