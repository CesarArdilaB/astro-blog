What is the significance of the 'doorknob conversation' in
medical insights?
Answer:The 'doorknob conversation' illustrates a common
behavior where patients often wait until the end of a
consultation to bring up sensitive topics, indicating how

discussing health issues.

community, relying heavily on interactions and discussions

connected to collective understanding and processes.
