3.Question
What is the significance of 'hate-watching' in relation to
sports teams and their fans?
Answer:'Hate-watching' refers to the phenomenon where fans
continue to support a team out of loyalty, even when they are
dissatisfied, encapsulating deep emotional ties. For the
Knicks, understanding this behavior provided insight into the
paradox of their fanbase: fans love the team but refuse to
invest financially until they see improvement. Tapping into
this idea can shape how they market their brand and engage
with their audience.

Answer:The concept of 'anger management' serves as an

team.
