Impact on Individuals and Society
Insights can induce personal change or choose to reinforce
existing beliefs. They can shape opinions and behaviors —
from reframing complex issues to modifying cultural
narratives. Insights can unify individuals and enhance
business relationships through shared understanding and
common ground, often evolving from private revelations to
public discussions.

Public Sharing of Insights
Insights that resonate often manifest publicly in
conversations, media, and culture, leading to a collective
engagement where individuals feel less isolated in their
experiences. This social sharing further increases the
visibility and applicability of insights in various realms.
Relating to People through Insights
Insights enable brands to connect with people by
encouraging self-reflection and identification. Consumers
often project their desired selves onto brands, illustrating
their longing for experiences that they may not currently
have.
Sharing Insights as a Survival Tool
Sharing insights serves not only as a transactional act but as a
means of building social capital and connection, contributing
to community and relational dynamics.

Awe and the Power of Insights
Insights can evoke feelings of awe, altering perceptions and
prompting personal reflections. They serve as cognitive
stimuli, resembling the comforting yet invigorating sensation
of a ‘wetty warmer’ in cold ocean waters, providing relief
and warmth through shared experiences.
Conclusion
While it’s possible to operate without insights, the pursuit
and effective handling of insights remain crucial in strategy,
akin to a martial artist knowing when to deploy techniques.
