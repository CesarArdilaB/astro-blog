5.Question
What role does the concept of 'obliquity' by John Kay
play in effective strategy formulation?
Answer:John Kay's idea of obliquity suggests that we
achieve goals more effectively when we pursue them
indirectly, allowing for flexibility and adaptation. In strategic
planning, this concept encourages leaders to remain open to
new opportunities and insights that arise along the journey
rather than rigidly adhering to a predetermined path, thus
fostering a more adaptive and responsive organizational
culture.
