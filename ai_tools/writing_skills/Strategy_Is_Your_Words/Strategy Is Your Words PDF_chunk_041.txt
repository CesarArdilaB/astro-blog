Operating Words
The Power of Words
Words shape your identity and influence your interaction
with others. The self-talk you engage in is vital, as it reflects
your experiences and needs, anchoring you in both your
career and public life.
Career Transition and Self-Awareness
Strategic careers are often transient. As your career evolves,
it’s important to maintain a sense of self that extends beyond
your job. Self-reflection and honoring your contributions are
essential for personal growth.
Words as Tools
Words are integral to your work, enabling you to engage with
the world. Embracing language in strategy not only enhances

[PÁGINA 125]

understanding but also respects human emotions and
connections.
The Nature of Strategy
Understanding strategy involves crafting opinions based on
information. Recognizing that selling is a part of the process
is crucial. Strategy should be approached with joy rather than
aggression.
The Variability of Language
Words can hold different meanings depending on context and
the speaker's intent. Communication can be wielded
positively or negatively, influencing dynamics in
professional settings.
Identifying Your Operating System
Every strategic effort exists within a specific context,
influenced by various external and internal factors.
Understanding your own operating system is key to effective
strategy formulation and implementation.
