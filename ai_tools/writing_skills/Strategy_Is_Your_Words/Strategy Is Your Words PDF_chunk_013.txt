Mischief Makes for Danger
The Nature of Mischief
Mischief embodies behavior that, while good-intentioned,
skirts the edge of bad. It is characterized by playful malice
and challenges conventional order in both profound and
trivial ways. A strategist's approach often reflects mischief by
revealing truths that disrupt the status quo.

[PÁGINA 39]

Order vs. Chaos
Order is resistant to change and adheres strictly to routine,
shunning anything seen as chaotic. When confronted by
threats to its established way of life, order becomes
suspicious and defensive, equating change with chaos. This
creates a struggle for strategists, who must navigate these
dynamics.
The Role of the Jester
The jester's function is to bring mischief into play. By daring
to express thoughts and truths that others might fear, the
jester challenges the rigidities of order. Embracing a range of
viewpoints, including cynicism and absurdism, enables the
jester to expose the flaws in conventional thinking.
The Struggle for Truth
Mischief encourages questioning established methods and
considering alternative approaches. However, this can be
disruptive and create turmoil in the strategist's life. The jester
must carefully balance the need for honesty with the
challenge of confronting one's own truths.

[PÁGINA 40]

Reframing Order
To succeed, the jester must find ways to redefine and
establish a new order. Change is often hidden under layers of
conventionality, and the search for a supportive community
becomes crucial in navigating these challenges. The jester's
existence is a continuous act of defiance against rigid
systems, wielding truth and ideas as weapons for
transformation.
