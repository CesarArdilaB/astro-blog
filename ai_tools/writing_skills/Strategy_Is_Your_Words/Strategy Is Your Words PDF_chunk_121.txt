5.Question
What does the silence in business scenarios signify and
how should one respond to it?
Answer:Silence in business often signifies indifference or a
lack of connection with the message being presented. To
respond effectively, one should look for any small sounds of
engagement, like snorts or chuckles, as they reveal a spark of
interest in what is being said, indicating a need to further
explore and provoke deeper discussion.
