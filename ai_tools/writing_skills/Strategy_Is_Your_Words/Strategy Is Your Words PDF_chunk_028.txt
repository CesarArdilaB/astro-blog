Advantages of Viewing Strategy as Art
Seeing strategy as an art form liberates the strategist. It
encourages a mindset shift where mistakes become
opportunities for learning, and every aspect of work is
viewed as a canvas for creativity. This view also positively
impacts the quality of strategic outputs, as it fosters a more
innovative atmosphere, aiding those seeking better strategies
and creative briefs.
Conclusion
Embracing strategy as an art form not only empowers the
strategist but also enhances the overall creative process in
business. This viewpoint allows for exploration and
experimentation, dispelling fears and limitations that come
from seeing strategy as a rigid science. The evolution
towards recognizing strategy as art is not just beneficial; it is
essential for thriving in a creative field.

Critical Thinking
Key Point:Strategy as an Art Form
Critical Interpretation:The contention that strategy
should be viewed as an art form invites scrutiny,
suggesting a challenge to conventional notions of
credibility and success in business. Pollard's view
implies that valuing creativity over empirical analysis
could empower strategists but may also risk
undermining the rigor of data-driven decision-making
that many industries rely upon. Detractors argue that
strategy must include scientific groundwork to mitigate
risks; for instance, insights from industry practitioners
(e.g., Lean Analytics by Benjamin Yoskovitz and
Alistair Croll) emphasize the importance of data in
creating effective strategies, potentially contradicting
Pollard's artistic stance and advocating for a balance
between creativity and analytics.
