Publishing Takes You Public
In the journey of strategy, the objective is to uncover
meaning and share it publicly, connecting more people with
clients. However, strategists often grapple with internal chaos
while focusing too narrowly on their professional image.
Understanding a more expansive public realm helps mitigate
the stress of internal comparisons.
The Importance of Going Public
Going public with one's strategy practice involves more than

[PÁGINA 64]

just enhancing one's visibility; it is about expressing
coherence and authentic intention. While some strategists
thrive without public exposure, many find growth and clarity
through sharing their work and insights. Courage in going
public can distinguish emerging strategists and guide
seasoned professionals towards their next steps.
Navigating the Checkbox Game
Young strategists often engage in a process aimed at meeting
predefined criteria for success, leading to superficial
connections. Genuine passion is expressed through active
contributions to the public discourse rather than merely
stating intentions. Demonstrating commitment by
showcasing public work leads to meaningful opportunities.
The Role of Feedback in Development
Going public functions as a learning mechanism, offering
vital feedback that reveals what the public desires. Like
comedians honing their craft through performance, strategists
benefit from the learning that emerges when exposing their
ideas to the world.
