What is the risk of treating strategy solely as a series of
reports with job titles?
Answer:Treating strategy merely as reports leads to a lifeless
approach where strategy lacks artistry and emotional
engagement. This transactional mindset stifles creativity and
can diminish the impact and effectiveness of strategic work.

2.Question
What role does conflict play in strategy presentations?
Answer:Conflict is crucial in strategy presentations as it can
generate interest and engagement. Every aspect of the
presentation, from meeting minutes to campaign launches,
reflects a dramatic arc where tension, stakes, and resolution
are explored. Strategists must embrace and articulate this
conflict to resonate with their audience.

team on an unorthodox field trip might stir unique

4.Question
What questions can help extract deeper insights during
interviews?
Answer:To extract deeper insights, ask open-ended questions
that probe emotions and personal experiences: "Why did this
come to mind right now? How do you feel about it? What
makes this situation significant to you?" This encourages
interviewees to share not just facts but their emotional
narratives, which can enrich strategic understanding.
