6.Question
What are the key takeaways from the distinction between
strategy and tactics as discussed in this chapter?
Answer:Key takeaways include recognizing that strategy
provides the roadmap for achieving a vision, while tactics are
the specific actions taken along that journey. Without a solid
strategy, tactics can lead to disjointed efforts that
compromise long-term objectives. Clarity of purpose and
actionable goals are essential for effective strategic planning.
